Description

Adobo is a Filipino traditional food.

It's mainly made out of pork or chicken, marinated soy sauce or vinegar which is the traditional adobo.

Ingredients

  • pork or chicken
  • soy sauce
  • oil
  • salt
  • onion
  • garlic
  • bay leaves
  • vinegar
  • black pepper
  • calamansi

Steps

  1. Heat oil over medium-high heat. Cook marinated meat without it sauce until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Transfer chicken to a plate and set aside.
  2. Add onion and garlic, cook until softened and brown, about 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Pour all the remaining marinated sauce.
  4. Return chicken to pan, increase heat to high, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until chicken is tender and cooked through, 35 to 40 minutes.
  5. Put on a plate and served.
